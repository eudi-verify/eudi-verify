# Code of Conduct

Be decent. Don't punch down.

## Do

- Give constructive feedback on work, not people
- Own your mistakes
- Respect different backgrounds and access needs

## Don't

- Harass or threaten anyone
- Use slurs or bigotry
- Share private info without consent
- Retaliate against reporters

Swearing about code is fine. Swearing at people isn't.

**Report issues:** mkasceldev@gmail.com

Violations may result in warnings, restrictions, or removal.
